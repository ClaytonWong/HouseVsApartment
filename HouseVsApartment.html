House Apartment
Positives
  • More chance of getting space for water tanks
  • More chance of getting solar panels, inverters and batteries
  • No strata fees to pay
  • Can get better security
  • Little to no need to do gardening
  • Less likely to be disturbed by strangers, such as door to door sales people
  • Less chance of getting junk mail
Negatives
  • Easier to break in
  • Need to do gardening
  • More likely to get disturbed by strangers, such as door to door sales people
  • You will get more junk mail
  • Need to pay strata fees
  • Strata fees can be expensive
  • Little to no room for water tanks
  • Little to no room for solar panels, inverters and batteries
